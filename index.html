Why did I take CSC 4720

I took Human Computer Interaction this semester because I wanted to learn more about creating systems and technologies for the user. Over the past few semester I have grown an interest in User experience design, and this class was the perfect opportunity for me to learn more about this field. As a future entreprenuer, I also think this is an important field to study, because in order for me to come up with my billion dollar idea, I have to uderstand who I am designing the product for, and how to go about catering the product for my clients. Then hopefully one day with a little bit of hard work (a lot!), a great idea, and great UX skills, the millions will come rolling in. Besides making me a great entreprenuer, I hope that the skills that I gain from this class help mould me into a better Software Engineer who not only can solve problems efficiently, but solves problems specifically for their user.
